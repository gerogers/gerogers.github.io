Tilt Puzzles

History

Tilt Puzzles is a variant of some of Andrea's tilt mazes. Andy Williams described an idea to me and I misunderstood it.
I wrote a Java Applet based on that misconception and Andrea designed a
set of puzzles for it © Andrea, 2002-2003.
The result was a set of Cups and Peas puzzles. This JavaScript incarnation accepts the same puzzle definitions.

Cups and Peas Aim

Fill both coloured cups simultaneously with peas of their respective colour.
Any black cups will be empty when both peas rest in their own cups.

Synchronised-tilt Mazes Aim

I have added support for Andrea's synchronised-tilt mazes (rendered a little differently). The aim here is to cover the coloured targets with balls of the same colour. Both targets must be covered at the same time. If two balls collide, the balls and targets all evaporate and you will need to reset the puzzle.

Controls

Use the Menu drop-list to select a puzzle (you may need to scroll the panel).

Buttons

Use the N E W S buttons to tilt the tray.
Use Actions/Reset to reset the current puzzle.

Size

You may wish to enlarge the play area.
Just drag the bottom right corner to change the size.
