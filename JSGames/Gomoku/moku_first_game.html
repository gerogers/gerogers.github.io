Gomoku - First Game

Return to index. To leave help, click on the Help drop-down menu.

At the start of the game you are faced with a 19 x 19 grid. This represents the traditional board (it is the same as a Go board) and pieces are played on the intersections rather than in the spaces between. The rows are given upper-case letters and the columns lower-case letters. These allow the intersections to be identified when describing the progress of a game.

Play your first move by clicking on an intersection. The computer will display your move and respond with a move of its own. The computer's last move is shown in pink rather than red but if another window is allowed to cover the play area, when the board is uncovered and is redrawn, this distinction will no longer be present. If this happens and you can't remember which was the last move, just press Undo followed by Redo.

Continue playing until one side completes 5 in a row. The computer will draw a line through a winning line. You can start a new game at any time by pressing New Game in the Actions drop down menu.
